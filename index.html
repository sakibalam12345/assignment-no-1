With their vibrant colors and sweet fragrances, flowers are among Mother Nature’s most beautiful creations. Out in the wild, flowers are an integral part of helping the environment around us. Inside our homes, we often use blooms to punctuate life’s memorable milestones—like the birth of a baby or a pick-me-up when something sad or upsetting occurs, as a get-well wish during an illness or a celebratory occasion such as a birthday or wedding, and especially as a token of condolence upon a loved one’s death.
